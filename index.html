About Us

Électrique Industrie L’Automatisme is a Moroccan company specialized in industrial electricity, automation systems, and electrical maintenance for factories and production sites. Led by Hakim and his skilled technical team, we provide complete, tailor-made solutions that meet the specific needs of each industrial client. Our expertise includes: Design and installation of electrical control panels PLC and HMI programming Automation and integration of production lines Preventive maintenance and on-site troubleshooting Our engineers and technicians combine precision, safety, and innovation to ensure efficiency and production continuity. With Électrique Industrie L’Automatisme, every project is a long-term partnership built on trust, quality, and professionalism.
